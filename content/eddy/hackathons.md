# Edward's Hackathons & Competitions

## The First Naga Mayoral Hackathon — Top 5 Finalist (February 2025)
Location: Naga City, Philippines. Top 5 finalist out of 200+ teams. Built SALBAR, an offline-first emergency health protocol for when internet, power, and smart city systems fail. The team was incubated into the program despite not taking the crown. Team qtr.zip: Edward, Eduardo, Abby, and Alexi. Hosted by Naga City Government and Mayor Leni Robredo.

## Ship or Be Shipped — 1st Runner Up (December 2024)
24-hour overnight hackathon by Build and Ship Philippines, hosted by Kaya Founders. Created Hakot, a platform that optimizes agricultural supply chains by utilizing empty trucks through AI-powered load matching for return trips. Built a cashless driver app using QRPH and PayRex. Team: Edward, Alexi, Eduardo, and Abby.

## IM Summit IT Business Case Competition — Top 10 (March 2024)
Location: Ateneo de Manila University. Proposed FORTRESS, a FIDO2-based risk observation, evaluation and security scoring system. Team: Edward, Gabe, Imman, Geline, and Andrew.
